# Obstetrics & Gynecology — Clinical Reference Knowledge

## Cardiotocography (CTG) Interpretation — NICE Guidelines
### Baseline Foetal Heart Rate (FHR)
| Category | Range | Classification |
|----------|-------|----------------|
| Normal | 110–160 BPM | Reassuring |
| Mild Bradycardia | 100–109 BPM | Non-reassuring |
| Severe Bradycardia | <100 BPM | Abnormal |
| Mild Tachycardia | 161–180 BPM | Non-reassuring |
| Severe Tachycardia | >180 BPM | Abnormal |

### Baseline Variability
- **Normal (Reassuring)**: 5–25 BPM bandwidth
- **Reduced (Non-reassuring)**: <5 BPM for 30–50 minutes
- **Absent (Abnormal)**: <5 BPM for >50 minutes
- **Sinusoidal**: Smooth undulating pattern, 3–5 cycles/min → severe fetal anemia

### Accelerations
- **Reactive**: ≥2 accelerations of ≥15 BPM lasting ≥15 seconds in 20 minutes
- **Preterm (<32 weeks)**: ≥10 BPM lasting ≥10 seconds is acceptable

### Decelerations
- **Early**: Mirror contractions, nadir at peak of contraction → head compression (benign)
- **Late**: Onset after contraction peak → uteroplacental insufficiency (concerning)
- **Variable**: Abrupt onset, variable shape → cord compression
- **Prolonged**: >2 minutes but <10 minutes → evaluate urgently

## CTG Classification (Overall)
| Category | Criteria | Action |
|----------|---------|--------|
| Normal | All features reassuring | Continue monitoring |
| Suspicious | One non-reassuring feature | Increased surveillance |
| Pathological | Two+ non-reassuring OR one abnormal | Urgent evaluation, consider delivery |

## Uterine Activity Monitoring
- **Normal**: ≤5 contractions in 10 minutes, averaged over 30 minutes
- **Tachysystole**: >5 contractions in 10 minutes, averaged over 30 minutes → risk of fetal compromise

## Foetal Kick Counting
- **Normal**: ≥10 movements in 2 hours during active period
- **Reduced**: <10 movements in 2 hours → non-stress test recommended

## Bishop Score (Cervical Readiness)
| Parameter | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 |
|-----------|---|---|---|---|
| Dilation (cm) | Closed | 1–2 | 3–4 | ≥5 |
| Effacement (%) | 0–30 | 40–50 | 60–70 | ≥80 |
| Station | -3 | -2 | -1/0 | +1/+2 |
| Consistency | Firm | Medium | Soft | — |
| Position | Posterior | Mid | Anterior | — |
- Score ≥8: favorable for induction. <6: cervical ripening recommended

## Red Flags Requiring Immediate Escalation
- FHR <100 BPM sustained >3 minutes (prolonged bradycardia)
- Absent variability with late/variable decelerations
- Sinusoidal FHR pattern → fetal anemia/hemorrhage
- Uterine tachysystole with FHR changes
- Absent fetal movements >12 hours
